英语写作

Posted on 2024-05-27 | In 专业英语词汇

Titles

四种标题类型：

  • full-sentence
  • nominal group
  • compound
  • question constructions

地学类文献主要使用 nominal group和compound结构。

举例

Nominal Group constructions

Estimates of olivine-basaltic melt electrical conductivity using a digital rock physics approach

Formation timescales of CV chondrites from component specific Hf–W systematics

Glacier velocity variability due to rain-induced sliding and cavity formation

Compound constructions

Cenozoic paleoaltimetry of the SE margin of the Tibetan Plateau: Constraints on the tectonic evolution of the region

Linking subsurface to surface degassing at active volcanoes: A thermodynamic model with applications to Erebus volcano

Lu–Hf geochronology on cm-sized garnets using micro sampling: New constraints on garnet growth rates and duration of metamorphism during continental collision (Menderes Massif, Turkey)

如何写一个好的标题？

  • 标题很少使用 the
    • 举例The effects of magmatic processes and crustal recycling on the molybdenum stable isotopic composition of Mid-Ocean Ridge Basalts
  • 标题中没有句号；两个部分使用冒号隔开，第二部分的首字母大写。
    • 举例Constraints on the timing and duration of orogenic events by combined Lu–Hf and Sm–Nd geochronology: An example from the Grenville orogeny
  • 正确使用修饰语(modifiers)。
    • 举例：during the Oligocene–Miocene transition
    • using a digital rock physics approach

如何使用介词

介词包括但不限于：

  • 简单介词: about, at, though, of, since等等。
  • 复合介词: into, onto等等。
  • 双介词: until after, from among等等。
  • 短语介词: according to, because of, in spite of等等。
  • 分词介词: regarding, concerning等等。

介词使用方式：

  • 说明属性
    • Cores were imaged using a combination of absorption-contrast and phase-contrast X-rayμ-CT at 27 keV to resolve the small density contrast between olivine and basaltic glass.
  • 用作状语(adverbial)
    • In the Transantarctic Mountains, the Ferrar is intruded into the mostly terrestrial Devonian to Triassic sedimentary deposits of the Beacon Supergroup.
  • 包含介词的搭配
    • 介词+名词+介词
      • However, the resulting succession of laminated crusts (thick red layerat the
        bottom of the snowpack in Fig. 7) was easily observable during field
        campaigns throughout the season.
    • 动词+介词
      • All of these models focus on constraining chemical equilibrium between a
        single melt and fluid.
    • 形容词+介词
      • The result of the model run is a list of possible combinations of gases and
        their proportions that, when combined, are equal to the composition of gas
        measured at the surface.

不要在某些情况下使用the:

  • 大陆名称，例如Asia,Africa
  • 大多数国家，除了the United States, the Netherlands
  • 城市、镇、州
  • 湖泊或海湾，例如Lake Titicaca
  • 山，例如Mont Qomolangma
  • 岛屿，例如Isles of Scilly，除了岛链Aleutians。
